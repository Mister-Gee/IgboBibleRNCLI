1 Chineke buru ayi ebe-nb͕abà na ike, O mewo ka achọta Ya n'iye aka nke-uku na nkpab͕u nile.
2 N'ihi nka ayi agaghi-atu egwu mb͕e uwa nāb͕anwe, Ma-ọbu mb͕e anēwezuga ugwu nile n'ọnọdu-ha ga n'etiti oké osimiri;
3 Mb͕e miri-ya nāb͕a aghara nāsu kwa ufufu, Mb͕e ugwu nile nēme nkpatu site na nbili-elu ya.Selah
4 Otù osimiri di, miri-iyi-ya nile nēme ka obodo Chineke ṅuria ọṅu, Bú ebe nsọ nke ebe-obibi nile nke Onye kachasi ihe nile elu.
5 Chineke nọ n'etiti ya; agaghi-ewezuga ya n'ọnọdu-ya: Chineke gēyere ya aka mb͕e chi bọrọ.
6 Mba nile ozo b͕ara aghara, ewezugara ala-eze nọnọdu-ha: O mere ka olu-Ya da, uwa nabaze.
7 Jehova nke usu nile nke ndi-agha nọyere ayi; Chineke nke Jekob buru ayi ulo-elu.Selah
8 Jenu, hu ọlu nile nke Jehova, Onye doworo ihe-iju-anya di iche iche n'uwa,
9 Nēme ka agha nile kwusi rue nsọtu uwa; Ọ nāb͕aji uta, b͕upia kwa ube; Ọ nēsure ub͕ọ-ala nile n'oku.
10 Ranu aka, mara kwa na Mu onwem bu Chineke: Agēbulim n'etiti mba nile, agēbulim n'uwa.
11 Jehova nke usu nile nke ndi-agha nọyere ayi; Chineke nke Jekob buru ayi ulo-elu.Selah

1 Umu-ndikom Livai; Giashon, na Kohat, na Merari.
2 Umu-ndikom Kohat kwa; Amram, na Izha, na Hebron, na Uziel.
3 Umu Amram kwa; Eron, na Moses, na Miriam. Umu-ndikom Eron kwa; Nedab, na Abaihu, na Eleeza, na Itama.
4 Eleeza muru Finehas, Finehas muru Abishua; 
5 Abishua we mua Buki, Buki mua Uzi; 
6 Uzi mua Zerahia, Zerahia mua Meraiot; 
7 Meraiot mua Amaraia, Amaraia mua Ahaitub; 
8 Ahaitub mua Zedok, Zedok mua Ahimaaz; 
9 Ahimaaz mua Azaraia, Azaraia mua Johanan; 
10 Johanan mua Azaraia, (ọ bu onye ahu bu onye-nchu-àjà n'ulo Solomon wuru nime Jerusalem:) 
11 Azaraia mua Amaraia, Amaraia mua Ahaitub; 
12 Ahaitub mua Zedok, Zedok mua Shalum; 
13 Shalum mua Hilkaia, Hilkaia mua Azaraia; 
14 Azaraia mua Seraia, Seraia mua Jehozadak; 
15 Jehozadak we je mb͕e Jehova mere ka Juda na Jerusalem je biri n'ala ọzọ n'aka Nebukadneza.
16 Umu-ndikom Livai; Giashom, na Kohat, na Merari.
17 Ma ndia bu aha umu-ndikom Giashom; Libni, na Shimai.
18 Umu-ndikom Kohat bu Amram, na Izha, na Hebron, na Uziel.
19 Umu-ndikom Merari; Mahli na Mushi. Ndia bu ab͕uru Livai dika ulo nke ndi bu nna-ha si di.
20 Nke Giashom; Libni nwa-ya, Jahat nwa-ya, Zima nwa-ya; 
21 Joa nwa-ya, Ido nwa-ya, Zera nwa-ya, Jeaterai nwa-ya.
22 Umu-ndikom Kohat; Aminadab nwa-ya, Kora nwa-ya, Asia nwa-ya; 
23 Elkena nwa-ya, Ebiasaf nwa-ya, na Asia nwa-ya; 
24 Tahat nwa-ya, Uriel nwa-ya, Uzaia nwa-ya, na Shaul nwa-ya.
25 Umu-ndikom Elkena kwa; Amasai, na Ahimot.
26 Ma Elkena: umu-ndikom Elkena; Zofai nwa-ya, na Nehat nwa-ya; 
27 Elaiab nwa-ya, Jeroham nwa-ya, Elkena nwa-ya.
28 Umu-ndikom Samuel kwa; ọkpara-ya, bú Joel, na nke-abua, bú Abaia.
29 Umu-ndikom Merari; Mahli, Libni nwa-ya, Shimai nwa-ya, Uza nwa-ya; 
30 Shimea nwa-ya, Hagia nwa-ya, Asaia nwa-ya.
31 Ndia bu kwa ndi Devid guzoro dika ndi-isi akuku abua nke ndi-ọbù-abù nke ulo Jehova, site na mb͕e ib͕e Chineke zuru ike.
32 Ha we nēje ozi n'iru ebe-obibi ulo-ikwū nzute n'abù, rue mb͕e Solomon wuru ulo Jehova nime Jerusalem: ha we guzo n'ibu ndi-isi òfùfè-ha dika ikpe ekpere ha si di.
33 Ndia bu kwa ndi nēguzo, ha na umu-ha ndikom. Nime umu-ndikom ndi Kohat: Himan ọbù-abù, nwa Joel, nwa Samuel; 
34 nwa Elkena, nwa Jeroham, nwa Eliel, nwa Toa; 
35 nwa Zuf, nwa Elkena, nwa Mahat, nwa Amasai; 
36 nwa Elkena, nwa Joel, nwa Azaraia, nwa Zefanaia; 
37 nwa Tahat, nwa Asia, nwa Ebiasaf, nwa Kora; 
38 nwa Izha, nwa Kohat, nwa Livai, nwa Israel.
39 Na nwa-nne-ya nwoke, bú Esaf, onye nēguzo n'aka-nri-ya, bú Esaf nwa Berekaia, nwa Shimea; 
40 nwa Maikael, nwa Baaseia, nwa Malkija; 
41 nwa Etni, nwa Zera, nwa Adaia; 
42 nwa Itan, nwa Zima, nwa Shimai; 
43 nwa Jahat, nwa Giashom, nwa Livai.
44 Umu-ndikom Merari, bú umu-nne-ha, nọ kwa n'aka-ekpe: Itan nwa Kishi, nwa Abdi, nwa Maluk; 
45 nwa Hashabaia, nwa Amazaia, nwa Hilkaia; 
46 nwa Amzi, nwa Bani, nwa Shemea; 
47 nwa Mahli, nwa Mushi, nwa Merari, nwa Livai.
48 Enye-kwa-ra umu-nne-ha, bú ndi Livai, ifè òfùfè nile nke ebe-obibi ulo Chineke.
49 Ma Eron na umu-ya ndikom nēsure ihe-nsure-ọku nēsì ísì utọ n'elu ebe-ichu-aja nke àjà-nsure-ọku, na n'elu ebe-ichu-aja nke ihe-nsure-ọku nēsì ísì utọ, bayere ije-ozi nile nke ebe nsọ kachasi ebe nsọ nile, na ikpuchiri Israel nmehie-ha, dika ihe nile si di nke Moses, bú orù Chineke, nyere n'iwu.
50 Ndia bu kwa umu-ndikom Eron; Eleeza nwa-ya, Finehas nwa-ya, Abishua nwa-ya; 
51 Buki nwa-ya, Uzi nwa-ya, Zerahaia nwa-ya; 
52 Meraiot nwa-ya, Amaraia nwa-ya, Ahaitub nwa-ya; 
53 Zedok nwa-ya, Ahimaaz nwa-ya.
54 Ma ndia bu ebe-obibi-ha dika ebe-ulo-ikwū nile ha si di n'ókè-ala nile ha: enyere umu Eron, bú ab͕uru ndi Kohat, n'ihi na ha nwere ife-nzà mbu, 
55 ha we nye ha Hebron n'ala Juda, na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile buruburu ya; 
56 ma ha nyere Keleb nwa Jefune ọhia obodo ahu, na obodo ntà ya nile.
57 Umu Eron ka ha nye-kwa-ra obodo nb͕aba, Hebron; na Libna na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile, na Jatia, na Eshtemoa na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile; 
58 na Hilen na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile, na Debia na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile; 
59 na Ashan na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile, na Bet-shemesh na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile: 
60 site kwa n'ebo Benjamin; Geba na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile, na Alemet na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile, na Anatot na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile. Obodo-ha nile bu obodo iri na atọ n'ab͕uru-ha nile.
61 Enye-kwa-ra umu Kohat, bú ndi fọduru, nime ab͕uru ebo ahu, nime nkera ebo ahu, bú nkera ebo Manase, obodo iri n'ife-nzà.
62 Enye-kwa-ra umu Giashom, dika ab͕uru nile ha si di, site n'ebo Isaka, site kwa n'ebo Asha, site kwa n'ebo Naftali, site kwa n'ebo Manase nime Beshan, obodo iri na atọ.
63 Enyere umu Merari, dika ab͕uru nile ha si di, site n'ebo Reuben, site kwa n'ebo Gad, site kwa n'ebo Zebulun, obodo iri na abua n'ife-nzà.
64 Umu Israel we nye ndi Livai obodo ndia na ebe-ita-nri-ha.
65 Ha we nye obodo ndia, nke ha kpọrọ aha, site n'ebo umu Juda, site kwa n'ebo umu Simeon, site kwa n'ebo umu Benjamin, n'ife-nzà.
66 Ma ufọdu nime ab͕uru umu Kohat nwere obodo ókè-ala nile ha site n'ebo Efraim.
67 Ha nyere ha obodo nb͕abà, bú Shekem na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile n'ugwu Efraim; Gezea kwa na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile; 
68 na Jọkmeam na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile, na Bet-horon na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile; 
69 na Ajalon na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile, na Gat-rimon na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile: 
70 site kwa na nkera ebo Manase; Anea na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile, na Bileam na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile ka enyere umu Kohat fọduru.
71 Enyere umu Giashom, site n'ab͕uru nkera ebo Manase, Golan nime Beshan na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile, na Ashtarot na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile: 
72 site kwa n'ebo Isaka; Kedesh na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile, na Daberat na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile; 
73 na Remot na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile, na Anem na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile: 
74 site kwa n'ebo Asha; Mashal na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile, na Abdon na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile; 
75 na Hukok na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile, na Rehob na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile: 
76 site kwa n'ebo Naftali; Kedesh nime Galili na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile, na Hamon na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile, na Kiriataim na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile.
77 Enyere umu Merari, bú ndi fọduru nime ndi Livai, site n'ebo Zebulun, Rimono na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile, na Teboa na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile: 
78 enye-kwa-ra ha n'ofè Jọdan n'akuku Jeriko, n'akuku ọwuwa-anyanwu Jọdan, site n'ebo Reuben, Beza n'ọzara na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile, na Jahza na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile, 
79 na Kedimot na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile, na Mefaat na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile: 
80 site kwa n'ebo Gad; Remot nime Gilead na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile, na Mahanaim na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile, 
81 na Heshbon na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile, na Jeza na ebe-ita-nri-ya nile.
